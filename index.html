Meet the Coach

Alex Jones is a highly regarded Performance Coach whose clients span from elite Olympic track runners to younger athletes seeking an opportunity to elevate their game and find success.

Alex Jones serves as a Director of Strength and Conditioning collegiately, has served as the Performance Coach for Olympic track runners at St. Augustine’s University, and has aided in the development of a strength training program for the football program at St. Augustine’s University. While serving as the Head Track and Field Coach at St. Augustine’s University, 18 athletes qualified for regionals and 12 qualified for the state championship competition.

Alex’s authentic, powerful, and engaging coaching has become increasingly recognized on the east coast as he has made the development of athletes his passion. You can learn more about Alex by following him on Social Media @iamtheperformancecoach.
